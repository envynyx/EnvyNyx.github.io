Technology should support your ambitions, not hinder them.

The goals of any technology used in business—from web applications to desktop software to kiosks and mobile apps—should be to make your business faster, to reduce overhead, to save time, to minimize risk, and to make employees and customers happier. We believe excellence in technology is best measured by the ratio between how much value it creates versus the time expended to create its value. Resonant Core provides software consulting services and develops business software solutions with excellence and security in mind.

Software Consultation

Resonant Core works with local businesses in Orlando (and the greater Central Florida area) to develop business solutions that are efficient, maintainable, and user-friendly.

Are there any time-sinks interfering with your productivity? Do you ever wish you could get a machine to perform a tedious task so you can focus on growing your company? If you said yes to either of these questions, our team of professional technology consultants can help. We are passionate about helping you face the challenges your business faces every day.

Application Development

The Resonant Core team is actively involved in cutting-edge application security research to help our customers and the community stay ahead of the latest threats.

Do you need a public website to attract more customers? How about an Intranet portal to streamline communications within your enterprise? From web to mobile, client to server, we develop solutions on any platform to make running your business less painful.

Code Auditing

How secure is the code that your business relies on? If someone were to read it with malicious intentions, would they uncover any flaws that would allow them to compromise your networks or steal sensitive information? We offer second opinions on software security and reliability as a service.
